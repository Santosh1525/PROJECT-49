Intersting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of the communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Intersting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigations abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Intersting Facts about Bear

  • Bears care deeply about family members.

Intersting Facts about Panda

  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.

  • Pandas eat fast, they eat a lot, and they spend about 12 hours a day doing it.

Intersting Facts about Flying Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Female cicadas lay 200 to 400 eggs.
  • Females cicadas may be attracted to the sound of motors.
